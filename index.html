Objectives

The 4th Computer Research and Engineering Symposium
commits itself to:

Promoting ICT awareness through exhibits and seminars;
Disseminating researches from different universities and colleges;
Establishing a community of IT enthusiasts;
Bridging the students and IT experts for better learning;
Acknowledging distinct researches;
Helping students to be updated about the new and emerging technologies;
Encouraging participants to imagine.
